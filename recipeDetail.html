INGREDIENTS
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 onions, diced
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 zucchini, diced
DIRECTIONS
  1. Heat olive oil in a large stock pot over medium heat. Add carrots, onions and salt and saute 5-10 minutes.
  2. Add zucchini, jalapeño, bell pepper, and chili peppers and continue sautéing 5 minutes.
  3. Add garlic and seasonings (including brown sugar) and saute 1-3 more minutes.
  4. Add broth, espresso, beer, beans, tomato paste, and diced tomatoes and simmer for 30 minutes-1 hour.
